Paving the Way to the Future

The future of Bokoni Platinum Mines holds immense promise as we embark on the ambitious mission of modernizing our operations for long-term sustainability. Central to this endeavor is the upgrading of mining processes and the implementation of innovative methods that ensure the mine's longevity while creating enduring job opportunities and fostering community development.

Over the next five years, we are prepared to make significant investments to realize this vision. Our goal is not just to meet industry standards but to surpass them, setting new benchmarks. We aim to build the mine to produce 240,000 tonnes per month, recognizing the vital importance of quantity in achieving the success of a platinum mine.

Our journey has already begun with early ounce production from the Middlepunt Area, the upgrade of the UG 02 Plant, and the ongoing development of Middelpunt. Our phased approach over the next three to five years will rely on specialized contractors and service providers to construct a new 240,000 tonne processing plant and sink two new declines, ensuring we meet production volumes.
