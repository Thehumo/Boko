CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

Rapid recruitment drive

Bokoni Platinum Mines is currently in a rapid recruitment phase as part of our extensive efforts to revive the mine, which had been inactive for six years. We invite applications from suitably qualified applicants with the required skills and experience to fill these roles in alignment with the minimum requirements specified in each job advertisement.

All positions will be stationed at Bokoni Platinum Mines in Atok, within the Fetakgomo Tubatse Local Municipality and Sekhukhune District Municipality in Limpopo.

The successful candidate's appointment is contingent upon meeting the medical certification requirements per the Mine Health and Safety Act 29/1996.

Preference to well-qualified local applicants:

Preference will be accorded to well-qualified local community applicants from designated groups in alignment with the Employment Equity Plan and/or Compliance Targets of the Organisation.
