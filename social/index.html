OUR RESPONSIBILITY

Community at our heart

Bokoni Platinum Mines has a dedicated Social Performance Department that serves as the vital link connecting our operations with our valued external stakeholders. Our commitment to fostering meaningful relationships is achieved through an approach characterised by openness, inclusivity, transparency, proactivity, and responsiveness.

Our core focus areas include Stakeholder Relations, Stakeholder Engagements, Socio-Economic Development (SED), Communication and Information-Sharing, as well as addressing Social, Health, and Environmental (SHE) and Risks and Impacts.

We are dedicated to ensuring that our engagements are built on trust and collaboration as we work together to promote sustainable and responsible mining practices.
