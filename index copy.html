Welcome to Bokoni Platinum Mines:
Shaping a Brighter Future

At Bokoni Platinum Mines (BPM), we're more than just a mining company; we're a catalyst for progress, guided by a commitment to excellence, sustainability, and community partnership. As a subsidiary of Africa Rainbow Minerals Limited (ARM), a leading South African mining conglomerate founded by the visionary Dr. Patrice Motsepe in 1997, we are proud to be a part of ARM's legacy of inclusivity and multiculturalism.

A Proud Heritage, A Bright Future

Bokoni Platinum Mines is situated in the picturesque Limpopo Province, where history converges with the promise of a brighter tomorrow. Our platinum mine, located on the eastern limb of the Bushveld Complex, sprawls across an impressive 15,000 hectares. It stands as South Africa's second-largest platinum group metals (PGMs) resource, firmly establishing BPM's significant presence in the industry.

Our journey has been marked by transformation and innovation. Despite facing financial challenges and a six-year period of care and maintenance, BPM's future took a positive turn in September 2022 when ARM acquired the mine from Anglo American Platinum and Atlatsa Resources Corporation. Today, ARM is the sole owner of Bokoni Platinum Mines, fully dedicated to its revival in collaboration with local communities.

A Commitment to Excellence

Revitalizing our operations is a substantial undertaking, requiring specialized work and substantial investment. However, these challenges only serve to ignite our determination to pioneer a new approach in the industry.

At BPM, we are unwavering in our commitment to greatness, placing quality, safety, and sustainability at the core of our mission. Our journey goes beyond mere resource extraction; it's about positively impacting the lives of our exceptional employees, their families, and the communities we proudly serve.
